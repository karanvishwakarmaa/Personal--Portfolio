Your Co-operative Bank, Online

Secure, reliable, and user-friendly online banking system for co-operative banks.

Project Overview

BANKONEER is a comprehensive software solution for co-operative banks, facilitating daily banking operations, online banking, and financial management through a user-friendly interface and sophisticated algorithms.

Key Features

User Authentication

Secure login and registration for both bank staff and customers.

Account Management

Create, view, deposit, withdraw, and manage accounts with ease.

Loan Management

Detailed view and management of ongoing and completed loans.

Transactions

Handle all types of transactions including third-party transfers.

Online Banking

Access to banking services through a web portal anytime, anywhere.

B-Payment Portal

Integrated online payment system for seamless third-party transfers.

Administrative Control

Full control over the system for adding, removing, and managing transactions and financials.

Technologies Used

- **Java**: For desktop workstation software used in administrative and banking operations.

- **PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript**: For developing the online banking platform.

- **MS-SQL with ODBC Connector Driver**: For database management.
